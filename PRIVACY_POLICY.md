# Privacy Policy for PBT Mobile

**Last Updated**: March 18, 2026

## Introduction

PBT Mobile ("we", "our", or "us") operates the PBT Mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
When you register for an account, we collect:
- Email address
- Full name
- Phone number
- Company information (for suppliers)
- Delivery address (for orders)

### Authentication Data
We use Firebase Authentication to secure your account:
- Email and password (encrypted)
- Google Account information (if you sign in with Google)
- Authentication tokens

### Transaction Data
When you use our marketplace, we collect:
- Order history
- Cart contents
- Payment information (processed securely through third-party providers)
- Supplier and retailer transaction records

### Usage Data
We automatically collect:
- Device information (model, OS version)
- App usage statistics
- Language preferences
- Search queries within the app
- Comparison lists and favorites

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- Create and manage your account
- Process orders and transactions
- Connect suppliers with retailers
- Provide customer support
- Send order confirmations and updates
- Improve app functionality and user experience
- Comply with legal obligations

## Data Storage and Security

- **Storage**: Your data is stored securely using Firebase Firestore and Firebase Storage
- **Encryption**: All data transmission is encrypted using industry-standard SSL/TLS protocols
- **Authentication**: Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text
- **Access Control**: We implement role-based access control (Supplier/Retailer/Admin)

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

We DO NOT sell your personal information. We may share your data with:

### Other Users
- Suppliers can see retailer company information for orders
- Retailers can see supplier contact information and stock details

### Service Providers
- **Firebase (Google LLC)**: Cloud hosting, database, and authentication
- **Expo**: App development and deployment platform

### Legal Requirements
We may disclose your information if required by law or to:
- Comply with legal processes
- Enforce our terms of service
- Protect rights, property, or safety

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access**: Request a copy of your personal data
- **Correction**: Update or correct your information
- **Deletion**: Request deletion of your account and data
- **Opt-out**: Unsubscribe from marketing communications
- **Data Portability**: Request your data in a portable format

## Data Retention

- **Active Accounts**: We retain your data while your account is active
- **Deleted Accounts**: Data is permanently deleted within 30 days of account deletion
- **Transaction Records**: Order history may be retained for up to 7 years for legal and accounting purposes

## Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18.

## International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside your country of residence, including the United States (Firebase servers). We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place.

## Third-Party Services

Our App uses the following third-party services:
- **Firebase**: [Firebase Privacy Policy](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy)
- **Expo**: [Expo Privacy Policy](https://expo.dev/privacy)
- **Google Sign-In**: [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by:
- Updating the "Last Updated" date
- Sending an in-app notification
- Posting a notice on our website (if applicable)
